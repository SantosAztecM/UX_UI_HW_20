Find your New Spark!

An application to help people find new hobbies, interact with others, and build upon their new passions while respecting their unique lives.

Research: 5 Interviews

For this application, we performed 5 qualitative interviews to build a more empathetic, comprehensive understanding of users' pain points with adopting and keeping up with a hobby.

Research: Ideal participants

Our ideal participants were between ages 18-35. We assessed how young adults find and maintain new hobbies, while asking about and understanding their frustrations and concerns about hobby upkeep.

Problem Statement

After our research and data gathering, we found that:

The Problem: Young open-minded, spontaneous adults need to be able to access a platform that offers a variety of hobbies based on their personal interests, preferences, schedule and budget because of not being able to commit to a hobby due to schedule, interest, and budget complications and challenges. We believe creating an app that provides access to a variety of hobbies based on young open-minded, spontaneous individuals’ interests, preferences and unique life demands will help them find and learn new hobbies. In addition, the app will also provide resources necessary for learning hobbies which will achieve making hobbies more accessible and enjoyable for any lifestyle or schedule.
